The Story

A decade at the center of the room.

For nearly ten years, Cosmic Ray was the heartbeat of one of New York's most storied stages. As Resident Band Leader and Promoter at B.B. King's Blues Club in Times Square, he booked the talent, hosted the nights, and led the house band — turning packed rooms into something closer to a revival.

Then B.B. King's closed its doors, and the project went quiet.

It's back.

Today, Cosmic Ray leads The Cosmic Blues Band — a flexible outfit that scales to the room without ever losing the fire. Solo for intimate listening rooms. A duo or trio for club dates. The full four-piece for rooms that can take the noise. The sound stays raw, groove-heavy, and unmistakably cosmic.

Stage & Tech

  • Self-contained PA (rooms up to ~75 cap)
